# captaining the team

The captains of a SciOly team can really make a huge impact on the competitive spirit of a team. When I was chosen as a captain for my senior year, I was really inspired by how the captains the year before had created such a tight-knit community. 

A lot of clubs both in high school and college end up feeling soulless if everyone's just there for an extracurricular or bullet on the resume -- I've never experienced that with SciOly. 

The leadership experience was also invaluable -- I think both the teamwork and disagreements/compromises were important learning opportunities, and we came out of it stronger <3
