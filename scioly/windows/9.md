# HUSO-India/Middle East

SciOly has also led to international opportunities for me! I was able to travel with HUSO to India and the UAE during my last two IAPs. We taught workshops, wrote exams, and got to explore the world (for free :D). 

I've been the earth science mentor for both camps, and I really love the opportunity to revisit such a cool field that I'm unfortunately not in anymore. The groups I've gone with have been amazing as well -- getting to make lifelong friends and memories is so cool!!

Teaching is super fun as well, and I've loved running workshops on things like knot theory, math of origami, and rocks/mineral identification! I hope to inspire a few kids to check out areas of science that they think are cool.
