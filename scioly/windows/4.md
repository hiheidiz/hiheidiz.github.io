# medaling at MIT

After junior year, I knew medaling at MIT SciOly in rocks was a possibility! I definitely studied a little too hard for rocks compared to my other events, but it was all worth it when MIT came around. This was also the first invite of the season, so we were excited to see how we actually stacked up against the rest of the field.

The test was super long and had a bajillion stations (we had never even practiced stations before), but we managed to sneak our way into 6th place for the medal omg. This was probably my best achievement ever in SciOly, even though it doesn't sound like anything crazy.
