# scioly PC and college volunteering

I found the transition from competing to planning pretty natural! Joining MIT Science Olympiad's planning committee was a dream of mine -- I wanted to see the behind the scenes of the most competitive invite in the country. It's pretty crazy how scuffed everything seems once you get on the other side LOL. Things like getting awards started on time goes from a simple problem to something that feels literally impossible. 

I started on the Competitor Logistics and Publicity committees but I think the way our club is structured makes it really easy to get a glimpse/even help out with other roles! I've done a bit of work with Finance, Software, and Events also, but my main thing is Pub now.

Besides MIT SciOly, I've also had the opportunity to volunteer at other tournaments like HUSO and MA States!
