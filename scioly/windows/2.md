# USESO camp!

My favorite event will always be Rocks and Minerals -- I have so so many fond memories of hours on the discord ID bot, taking dozens of practice tests, and never quite learning thin sections and phase diagrams properly. 

Thanks to the rocks grind, I was able to qualify for and attend the USESO (basically earth science olympiad) Training Camp (summer after junior year). I didn't know what was going on in any section besides rocks, but that's okay.
